# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales data to extract meaningful business insights using Power BI. The dashboard helps in understanding sales performance across regions, categories, and products.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze sales and profit trends
* Identify top-performing products
* Understand regional performance
* Detect loss-making areas

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* Data Cleaning & Data Analysis

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Superstore Sales Dataset
* Contains order details, sales, profit, region, and product information

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Total Sales, Profit, and Orders (KPI Cards)
* Sales by Region
* Sales by Category
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Top 5 Products by Sales
* Profit/Loss by Sub-Category
* Interactive Filters (Region & Category)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* West region generates the highest sales, while South is the lowest
* Technology category contributes the most revenue
* Sales peak in November (seasonal trend)
* Canon imageCLASS is the top-selling product
* Tables sub-category results in the highest loss

---

## 💡 Business Impact

* Helps businesses identify high-performing areas
* Supports decision-making for inventory and marketing
* Highlights loss-making products for cost optimization

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This dashboard provides a clear and interactive way to analyze business performance and supports data-driven decision-making.
